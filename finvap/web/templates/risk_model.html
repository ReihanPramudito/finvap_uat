Risk model

These are the weights each asset‑context tag applies to the CVSS environmental score. The mechanism (CR/IR/AR, Modified Attack Vector) is the CVSS v3.1/v4.0 environmental spec itself; the shipped default levels follow FIPS 199's impact definitions and NIST SP 800‑60's information‑type mappings (full citation chain: docs/SCORING.md §5). Edit a value to tune how your house scores risk, then Recompute to apply it to every finding. Reset returns to the shipped defaults. Changes are recorded in History; the report is never affected.
